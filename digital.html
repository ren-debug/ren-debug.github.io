With the adoption of Salesforce, I collaborated with the CRM team to facilitate their involvement in newsletter design using templates that incorporated various modules. My contribution included creating numerous modules based on a responsive 9 x 9 grid, followed by thorough testing using Litmus, and finally integrating them into Salesforce via Content Builder.

This encompassed various aspects, from campaign management to opt-in and opt-out processes, segmentation, and retargeting efforts. My role primarily focused on supporting the team's objectives and ensuring the successful integration of Salesforce and Content Builder into our workflow.
