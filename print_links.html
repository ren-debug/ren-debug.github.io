As the appointed Art Director of the rebranding for Capital Gardens, I was responsible for establishing a consistent and coherent brand identity across all eight garden centres. The brand incorporates the materiality of plants and home goods into its visual identity, with a focus on engaging the customer through the use of tactile materials, and printing methods such as foiling and embossing.
