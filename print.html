One of the most exciting projects I worked on for McArthurGlen involved me leading the implementation of Salesforce and Content Builder. Salesforce is an innovating online tool that revolutionized the way work was distributed within our department. With this tool, the CRM team was able to take over the design of newsletters through templates combining various modules. I created a large number of modules based on a fully responsive 9 x 9 grid, tested the result on Litmus and converted it on Salesforce through Content Builder. Throughout the entire process, I worked closely with the CRM team to ensure a consistent and seamless customer journey between CRM and Paid Media. This included everything from the campaign, opt-in and opt-out processes, segmentation, and re targeting.
