about

I'm Irèn Spalletti, a Visual Designer and Front-End Developer with more than 12 years of experience. My passion for Visual Communication spans across both print and digital media, and it has made me appreciate the physical feel and look of materials. This unique perspective gives me an edge when crafting designs for the digital realm, which constantly excites and inspires me with its endless possibilities.

My journey has allowed me to grasp the fundamentals of traditional design principles, making it effortless for me to bring ideas to life across various platforms. My portfolio is all about attention to detail, offering a clean and timeless style. I'm all about putting typography at the forefront, creating designs that effortlessly blend elegance and functionality.
